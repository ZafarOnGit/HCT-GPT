#HCT-GPT

HCT-GPT is a specialized Generative Model designed to be the definitive academic resource for students and faculty at the Higher Colleges of Technology (HCT).

The model has been exclusively trained using the official HCT course materials and curriculum data. Its purpose is singular: to provide high-precision, contextually-relevant answers tailored to any course taught at the college. 

This model is designed to serve as a focused, domain-specific resource for HCT students and faculty, offering replies oriented to the content and curriculum of HCT's course material.
